for the actual modelling or making part, first we start by cutting the foam board in dimensions in blueprint.
for cutting, since foam board is soft, we can easily do it with a cutter...not much of a hassle.
just make sure that the aerofoil shape is not altered for left snd right parts, otherwise plane can become unstable.

so after cutting we will join and parts and give it the shape of a plane...for that you can see 3D files...for how each part joins
and which part joins to what...for joining I will be using superglue and hot glue gun...as they are fast and effecient.
since it is a foamboard, typical screws won't work.

now for the servos attachment...the ailerons servos would be attached inside the left and right wings respectively and elevator and rudder 
will be inside the plane to balance centre of gravity...exact placement will be updated after parts arrive.

also for the hinges we will make a typical V-cut...it allows both strength and movement...and then apply silicone hinge.
if you see the youtube video I referenced to, you will gget to know.

and the thrust motor, that will be attached at the nose, the holes for it will depend on which motor you use.

a thing to worth note is that we should put the communication module or atleast its antinnea should be out of the frame for
maximum range. and the imu should be in the geometric center of the plane...I think it will be around near middle of the wing to trailing edge 
of the wing. again the exact placement and other things will be mentioned after parts arrive...also there will be journals.
